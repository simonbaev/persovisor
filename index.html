About PersoVisor

A fast and easy way to make the right decision

Personal decision making adVisor is a compact web application designed to help you to choose among a number of most favorable options. It takes into account psychological patterns that are identified on the basis of input provided by a user.

How does it work?

On the Personalization tab (next one to the right) you will need to specify an arbitrary set of options that you are hesitating to choose one from. At that point you will only need to name them, such that later on, on the Play tab, you will be able to destinct them by name. Tabs can be clicked (tapped), or, in case you are working on a mobile device (phone, tablet, phablet, etc) you can also do a swipe gesture to the left or to the right to appropriately switch the tab.

During the play phase you will need to think about each option as having its own pros and cons with corresponding level of intencity and likelihood to happen.

Each of both metrics, for instance intencity and likelihood, has to be associated with a level, measured from low to high, that can be set by dragging corresponding slider handle. Value of the metric can be controlled by observing color of the slider bar and associated label.
